PESEL Reader

← Projects

A small program for reading PESEL numbers.

 Details

Status Mature
Language C
License 3-clause BSD
Role Maintainer
Download GitHub Bug Tracker

This ve­ry small pie­ce of so­ftwa­re de­co­des PE­SEL num­bers (think SSNs for Po­li­sh ci­ti­zen­s) in­to C structs. The fol­lo­wing in­for­ma­tion is en­co­ded in a PE­SE­L num­ber (11 di­gits lon­g; the po­si­tions are ze­ro­-in­de­xe­d):

  • Da­te of Birth (po­si­tions 0-5)
  • Gen­der (po­si­tion 9)
  • Check­sum (po­si­tion 10)

The PE­SEL Re­ader is ba­sed on the of­fi­cial spe­ci­fi­ca­tion, as pu­bli­shed by the Mi­ni­stry of the In­te­rior of the Re­pu­blic of Po­land.
